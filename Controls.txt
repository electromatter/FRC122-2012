Joystick 1 - Right Joystick - Drive Joystick
Joystick 2 - Left Joystick - Operator Joystick

Arcade drive is the default drive mode.

Drive Joystick Controls:
 Buttons:
  Trigger - Shoot
 Axis:
  Y Axis - Arcade Throttle/Tank Left Throttle
  X Axis - Arcade Turning
Operator Joystick Controls:
 Axis:
  Y Axis - Tank Right Throttle
  Nub X Axis - Shooter Angle
  Nub Y Axis - Camera Ajustment
  Throttle - Shooter Speed
 Buttons:
  Trigger - Shoot
  7 - Camera Light Toggle
  8 - Lift Toggle
  9 - Shooter Toggle
  10 - Arcade Drive/Tank Drive Toggle
  11 - Arm Up
  12 - Arm Down
